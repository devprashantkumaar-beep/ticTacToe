About Tic Tac Toe Pro

Tic Tac Toe Pro is a premium, lightweight, and offline-first gaming web application designed for mobile devices and desktops alike. Built using 100% vanilla HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, it loads in milliseconds, uses zero framework bloat, and provides high-value educational content alongside clean gameplay.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Tic Tac Toe Pro operates fully on the client-side. We do not track, collect, or store your personal information on external servers. All statistics (wins, losses, streaks) and solved puzzle indices are stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage. If you clear your browser cache, your statistics will reset. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

Terms of Use

This platform is provided for entertainment and educational purposes free of charge. Users may play against our AI models or solve interactive puzzles unlimited times. Commercial use or replication of the puzzle database maps must comply with open-source licensing rules.

Accessibility Support

Our game board and interface elements conform to WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards. We use high-contrast text tags, focus-visible outlines, skip links, and ARIA labels so that screen readers and keyboard users can easily navigate the grids and controls.
